Participating Remotely

If you're unable to join one of the local sites, that's OK! In addition to watching the broadcast sessions, there are two other ways for you to participate from anywhere on the web:

  • Remote participants are welcome to convene a session themselves; please email the Mentoring Committee with your idea, and we'll get you on the list.
  • Many sites will be making time for participants to practice new lessons and get feedback from their peers; if you'd like a chance to teach, contact a site in your timezone by following the link in the pin on the map above, or contact the Mentoring Committee and we'll help put you in touch.
